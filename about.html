Naomi Delores Spence Lovelace was born on January 1, 1945 in Dunn, North Carolina. Naomi Lovelace is the second youngest daughter to the late Luther Spence and the late Katie Spence. Naomi Lovelace spent most of her life in church settings and loves to fish. She Taught biology and science at Broughton High School and was even apart of the discipline committee, where she was in supervising children in detention. After 36 years of teaching at Broughton High School. Naomi Lovelace retires from teaching. She met her late husband, Rev. Dr. Joseph C. Lovelace and got married in June 1965. The married couple are blessed with three children, Derrick Lovelace, Keischa Lovelace, and Brittani Lovelace. She also has three grandchildren, Alyssa Wise, Christina Lovelace, and Ericka Lovelace. Naomi is a amazing mother, grandmother and great-aunt to her family. We wish her a Happy 80th Birthday and we wish her love and happiness for many years to come.
